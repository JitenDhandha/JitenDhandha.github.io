JWST high-\(z\) literature [deprecated]

This is a (probably incomplete) list of high-\(z\) observational papers, made in light of the flurry of exciting observations and results from the JWST. As we push the limits of how deep we can probe into space, the definition of "high redshift" gets muddy. This list is intended for roughly those papers/observations concerning galaxies at \(z\gtrsim7\).

The list is in reverse-chronological order of the first arXiv date of submission (so latest papers first), and can be filtered using the checkboxes below to categorize papers on the basis of the fields they include in the study.

If you find a paper missing, please tweet at or directly message either me @currysmologist or Christian Kirkham @1420megahertz. Alternatively, you can also email me at jvd29@cam.ac.uk.
